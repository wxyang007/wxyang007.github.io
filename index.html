We participated in the Great Backyard Bird Count!

@Desert Botanical Garden, Pheonix, Arizona

March 10, 2022 Wenxin Yang

My friends and labmates and I went to Desert Botanical Garden to participate in the Great Backyard Bird Count organized by Cornell Ornithology Lab. We spotted many bird species (including a family of Gamble's Quail, many Mourning Doves, and many others)and also saw a fox and some rabbits.
