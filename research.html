New paper on connectivity metrics and protected areas

Percentage of area protected can substitute for more complicated structural metrics when monitoring protected area connectivity.

December 14, 2023 Wenxin Yang

New paper with Dr. Amy Frazier and Dr. Peter Kedron: We find the percentage of area protected can be a viable proxy for many more complicated structural connectivity metrics in global target reporting, creating easier pathways for monitoring.The paper is open access in Ecological Indicators with code available on GitHub. Information of metric equations and interpretations also available in the supplemental material.
